How I got into design

I started my career in marketing, helping to optimize websites for SEO. That led to web development, and I started building websites for side clients. The problem was that all my websites were ugly. So, I bought Sketch and started to learn web design.

I quickly learned that it is way more fun to design products. So I started to pick up side clients doing that. After doing that for a few years I turned the side hustle into my day job.
